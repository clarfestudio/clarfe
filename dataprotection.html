Personal
Data
Protection Policy

CLARFE Digital Marketing Agency, committed to transparency, security, and privacy of its users, provides this Data Protection Policy to inform how your personal data is collected, used, stored, and protected, in compliance with the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (LGPD - Law No. 13.709/2018) and its updates until 2025.

1.
Personal Data
Collected

When filling out our contact form, we collect the following personal data:

  • Full name;
  • CPF (Brazilian Tax ID);
  • Email;
  • Phone and WhatsApp;
  • Profession;
  • Message with the reason for contact;

This data is voluntarily provided by you and is essential for us to assist you properly.

2.
Purpose of
Processing

The information provided will be used for the following purposes:

  • Contacting you through our commercial team to handle requests for quotes, proposals, and clarifications about our services;
  • Sending communications related to our services, including emails, WhatsApp messages, updates, offers, informational content, and commercial and marketing actions;
  • Improving our communication with you in a personalized way based on your demonstrated interest;
  • Compliance with legal or regulatory obligations when applicable.
3.
Legal Basis for
Processing

The processing of your personal data is based on the following legal grounds provided by LGPD:

  • Consent of the data subject, provided freely, informed, and unequivocally;
  • Legitimate interest of CLARFE, in developing its commercial and marketing activities, respecting the fundamental rights and freedoms of the data subject.
4.
Data
Sharing

CLARFE does not share, sell, or transfer your data to unauthorized third parties. Occasionally, your data may be shared with service providers contracted by CLARFE, exclusively for technical operation purposes, always under contractual confidentiality and security obligations.

5.
Storage and
Security

Your data is stored in a secure environment with restricted access and appropriate technologies to protect information against unauthorized access, leaks, loss, or alteration. Data will be maintained for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes above or until eventual revocation of consent.

6.
Your Rights

Under LGPD, you have the right to:

  • Confirm the existence of processing;
  • Access, correct, update, or request deletion of your data;
  • Revoke consent at any time, without prejudice to the legality of previous processing;
  • Request data portability and limitation of its use;

To exercise your rights, contact us by email: clarfe@clarfe.com.br

7.
Consent

By submitting your data through this website, you declare that you have read, understood, and agree with this Data Protection Policy, expressly consenting to the processing of your information under the terms described herein.
